About AFL

What Is AFL?

African League (AFL) is a competitive Mobile Legends: Bang Bang esports initiative built to develop, connect, and elevate African players and teams.

Our mission is to create a structured esports ecosystem across Africa, enabling players to compete at high levels while gaining recognition on the global stage.

Built for competition, community, and continental scale
01

Structured Ecosystem

Build a structured African MLBB competitive ecosystem with clear pathways and standards.

02

Cross-Country Collaboration

Strengthen collaboration between African esports communities through shared competition.

03

Server Advocacy

Advocate for a dedicated African server to unlock fair, stable, high-level play.

04

Global Representation

Increase African representation in international tournaments and global esports narratives.

05

Long-Term Growth

Support lasting player, team, and organizational development beyond a single season.
